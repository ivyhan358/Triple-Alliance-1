A Scenic December Attraction In Pyin Oo Lwin

December Garden

December Garden, in Pyin Oo Lwin, was created as a scenic garden for flower displays and festivals. Its first major public event was the 2019 December–January Flower Festival, showcasing thousands of plants and artistic floral arrangements to promote tourism, recreation, and environmental awareness. It complements the older Kandawgyi Botanical Gardens as a modern attraction for visitors.

B.E Waterfall

  • Originally called Hampshire Falls during the British era.

  • Later renovated by the Burma Engineer Corps (B.E.), which is why it became known as B.E. Fall.

  • Over time, it turned into a popular picnic and relaxation spot for locals and visitors.

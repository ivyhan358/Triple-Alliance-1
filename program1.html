Our Programs

The various parks in Pyin Oo Lwin often attract lots of visitors and it is important for these visitors to enjoy themselves when visiting. To achieve this various types of facilities and equipment are required. These facilities and equipment serve a wide range of purposes and is how some parks make revenue.

Happy & Tidy

  • Dispose of waste in the correct bins (general, recyclable, compostable)

  • Participate in park clean-up drives and community activities

  • Use reusable containers and reduce single-use plastics

  • Keep picnic areas tidy and leave no food scraps behind

  • Encourage friends and family to practice cleanliness

  • Plant trees or help maintain flowerbeds and green spaces

Happy & Tidy

  • Throw rubbish in bins and recycle properly

  • Keep noise low and respect others peace

  • Use facilities and playground equipment safely and responsibly

  • Walk on pathways and protect plants, flowers, and animals

  • Clean up after yourself and keep the park tidy

  • Follow park rules, report problems, and respect other visitors

Common Issues & Problems

The varous parks located in Pyin Oo Lwin attracts hundreds of thousand visitors each year. This influx of visitors can often result in various problems & issues. These issues and problems if not addressed properly may cause more problems to arise, which can negativly effect the park itself as well as the visitors alike.

Problems & Issues

  • A lack of tourisms in some parks

  • Lack of awareness, care & responsibility of proper waste management

  • Some parks are not properly maintained

  • Not enough bins for the people to properl dispose waste

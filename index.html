Preserving Local Park in Pyin Oo Lwin

“Together, we build a cleaner and healtheir community”

Our Goal & Purpose

Empowering our community to protect and improve our park by using web technology, promoting sustainability, and encouraging collaboration, so that together we can create a cleaner, safer, and greener environment for the future.

Why is it improtant?

Preserving and revitalizing our local park is essential because it helps protect the environment, improves community well-being, and ensures that public spaces remain clean, safe, and accessible for everyone. A healthy park provides fresh air, supports local plants and wildlife, and creates a peaceful area for exercise, learning, and social activities. By working together to maintain our park, we build community pride, encourage responsible citizenship, and secure a green and sustainable future for the next generation.
